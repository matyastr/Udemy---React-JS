My Trips

Maui, Hawaii

I travelled with my wife and friends to Maui in May of 2019. It was one of the most amazing trips ever! My highlight was the road to Hana, a day-long trip around the eastern half of the island. We stopped at some amazing waterfalls to swim!

Juneau, Alaska

I travelled with my fiance (then) to Alaska in May of 2018. Seeing the glaciers and light all evening were unforgettable!

Disney Cruise, Bahamas

Disney cruises are some of my favorite vacations. The relaxation you get and level of service can't be matched!

Coimbatore, India

Being able to stay with a family in rural India shed light on a once-in-a-liftime experience. I will never forget the smell of tea from the plantations and the taste of the homecook meals.

Italy

In May 2017, I travelled for 2 weeks in Italy to study Lean Six Sigma. While not in class, I travelled a lot. I went to Verona, Venice, Rome, Florence, and Pompeii. The ruins of Pompeii were chilling and incredible to see.

Munich, Germany

In my second trip to Europe, I got the opportunity to visit Germany and the city of Munich. The highlights of this trip included a stop at the original Hofbrauhaus, the Olympic Park, and the incredible food! I also had the opportunity to visit the Dachau concentration camp. It was another chilling and humble moment.

Geneva-on-the-Lake, Ohio

Geneva-on-the-Lake will always hold a special place in my heart. I have been there a couple of times now and enjoy every time I get up there.

Smokey Mountains, North Carolina

In 2016 I went on a camping trip to the Great Smokey Mountains. It was such a nice trip. The sunrises and scenery were beauituful. I also walked 10 miles on the Appalachian Trial and went to a great overlook called Charled Bunion

Brussels, Belgium

Last but not least is Brussels, Belgium. I have had the opportunity to travel to Brussels twice now. Every time I find something new to enjoy! It is such a great city and I know so many friends who live there!
